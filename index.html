About

Theo has been designing websites for several years, with experience in WordPress, HTML, CSS and Javascript. The latter three allow him to deliver a faster and more flexible website, unlike other design agencies who only use WordPress.

Speed is incredibly important to Google and other search engines. It's also important to users; 40% abandon websites that take more than 3 seconds to load (KissMetrics). Our latest website, Marlborough Structural Design, has a load time of 0.7 seconds (see audit). That's over seven times faster than the average 2020 page speed, allowing them to achieve near top rankings.

Our websites also compete well in Google's Lighthouse test, with the same site, we achieve an outstanding score of 99% (see audit). If you google 'Structural Engineer Marlborough' (go to search), the first result is a big yellow-page type company, closely followed by our client's website.
